## what, why, how - project management
project management is the application of knowledge, skills, tools, and techniques to meet the project requirements and achieve the desired outcome.


project management can save money and time
- 48% of projects miss delivery dates
- 43% of project miss budgets targets
- 31% of projects fail to meet an organization goals

# project manager what do they do
- planning and organizing
	- gathering requirements from teamates or customers
	- creating plans
- managing tasks
	- for the team members
	- communicates key milestones to the large team or stakeholders
- budgeting
- controlling costs and other factors
they make sure that the project is delivered in time and on budget

the responsibilities may vary depending on the project 

the company role name could be like Operations IT Manager, instead of project manager outright
- operations assistant
- program assistant
- project coordinator
- project analyst
- project controller

project managers manage a group of projects that are related or similar to one another
portfolio manager are responsible for managing a group of related programs within the same organization
